About Me

Hi! I'm married and have one child, who just turned seventeen. I'm a Technical Communication major at EWU. My dream is to write stories for a living, but until then, I enjoy helping other people become better writers. I'm what you'd call an "armchair tourist;" I prefer using my imagination to actually traveling, but still enjoy meeting new people.

I love to read just about anything; my Kindle is never far from my side! I grew up devouring the works of Isaac Asimov, Ray Bradbury, and Michael Crichton. Some of my favorite contemporary authors include S. G. Redling, Elizabeth Rose, and Joe Hill.

Second only to books is my love of cooking. I think that my best dishes are anything made primarily from eggs. My cheese souffle is legendary! I can't imagine a more fun way to spend a weekend than to have my friends over for a delicious Sunday brunch with plenty of mimosas to go around. Cheers!
