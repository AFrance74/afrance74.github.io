About Me

I am a technical communicator. I love science and technology, and I love writing about science and technology. I understand the lingo of business, web development, graphic design, and Star Trek. I even speak a little German. I want to tell the world the story of where we are, how we got here, and where we're going. I would love to help you tell your story.

I have been married for seventeen years. My husband works in Spokane Valley, and our daughter is in her junior year at Gonzaga Preparatory School. I am not a native of Spokane, Washington, but I have always been very fond of the area, with its forest-covered mountains and rolling hills.
